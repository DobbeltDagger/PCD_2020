Live Coding with Sonic Pi

With Rolf Holm & Frederik la Cour. In this workshop, you will be introduced to live-coding as a performance practice, while being taught the basics of Live Coding. The workshop will be focusing on the Live Coding language Sonic Pi. No prior coding knowledge is required to attend. Bring your laptop, headphones/earbuds and your good spirits.
